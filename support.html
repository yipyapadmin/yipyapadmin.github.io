YipYap Support

Need help with YipYap, want to report a bug, or have a question about your account?

Contact

Email: support@yipyapwin.com

When contacting support, please include your device type, app version if available, and a short description of the issue.

For account deletion requests, see the Account Deletion Requests section below.

Reporting Content or Users

You can report polls, comments, profiles, images, and other user-generated content using the reporting controls inside YipYap. Reports may be reviewed by YipYap administrators.

If you cannot use the in-app reporting tools, email support@yipyapwin.com and describe the content or account involved.

Account Deletion Requests

You can request deletion of your YipYap account and associated account data.

Email support@yipyapwin.com with the subject YipYap Account Deletion Request. Include the email address used for your YipYap account.

YipYap may need to verify account ownership before deleting the account. Requests will be reviewed and processed as required by applicable policies or law.

Request Account Deletion

Age and Content Access

YipYap is for users age 13 and older. Users under 13 are blocked from using app features. Accounts for users age 13–17 have mature-topic filtering enabled.

Your birthday is used privately for age verification and is not shown on your public profile. See the Privacy Policy for more information.

Tester Feedback

If you are testing YipYap, please explore multiple features including answering polls, creating polls, viewing results, account setup, profile settings, reporting content, and sharing any bugs you notice.
