YipYap Child Safety Standards

YipYap is a 13+ social polling app. YipYap is not directed to children under 13.

YipYap prohibits child sexual abuse material, sexual exploitation of minors, grooming, sexual solicitation of minors, and any content or behavior that endangers children.

YipYap provides in-app reporting tools so users can report unsafe content, users, comments, polls, images, or child safety concerns.

Reported content may be reviewed and removed. Accounts that violate child safety rules may be restricted, suspended, or banned.

YipYap uses safety measures including age gating, mature-topic filtering, text moderation, image moderation, user reporting, and moderation review tools.

When required by law, YipYap may report child safety concerns or child sexual abuse material to the appropriate regional or national authorities.
