We use reasonable measures to protect user information. However, no online service can guarantee complete security.

Children

YipYap is not intended for children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
